IMPORTANT INFO

  • I cannot take requests to draw specific motives; I, too, have no idea what my hands will draw... thus, the ultimate art gacha.
  • The base colour is black, but you can choose one or two additional colours if you prefer.
  • My scanner is funky, so more often than not I will use my phone to take a picture of the finished pattern, and crop + clean them to a suitable quality. Thus, I cannot provide very high-resolution files.
  • Commissions are only open when it's up on Party Finder, unless I specifically mention otherwise.
  • I am completely new to doing commissions. My irl has nothing to do with art, but I want to try this out and see where it leads!
  • I might use / post the commissioned piece on other social media or for other purposes (mayhaps a future portfolio).
  • I am often told that these patterns make good tattoos... feel free to do so!
  • This is completely dependent on goodwill, but I hope that the commissioned pieces will not be used for commercial purposes. Feel free to share them around, or even modify them to your liking, making different iterations... add more colours and squiggly lines... ah, but maybe those are my own wishes. Let me know if you do so!
  • Lastly, I live in Kujata!
